# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:
- Harassing language or imagery.
- Personal attacks.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting **palermoinsta1@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces and events.
